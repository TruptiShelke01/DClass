MHT-CET

MHT CET, or Maharashtra Common Entrance Test, is a state-level entrance exam conducted by the Government of Maharashtra for admission to undergraduate courses in engineering, pharmacy, and agriculture.

It serves as a gateway for admission to various colleges across the state. The exam covers subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology, and tests students' knowledge and aptitude.

MHT CET follows a syllabus based on the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education.

HSC/SSC

HSC (Higher Secondary Certificate) are crucial milestones in the academic journey of students in Maharashtra. HSC exams are conducted for 12th-grade students.

SSC (Secondary School Certificate) SSC exams are for 10th-grade students

Both exams are administered by the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE). The HSC exam covers various streams like Science, Commerce, and Arts, while the SSC exam assesses fundamental subjects like Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Languages.
